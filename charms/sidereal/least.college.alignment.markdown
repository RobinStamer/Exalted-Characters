#Least (College) Alignment
**Cost:** 1c; **Mins:** (College) 4, Essence 3; **Type:** Reflexive<br />
**Keywords:** Combo-Ok, Charges<br />
**Duration:** One Scene<br />
**Prerequisite Charms:** Linked Propitious (Ability) Alignment

*Like motes, Charges may be commited and will reduce the maximum amount of Charges one can have at any one time.*

FLUFF FLUFF FLUFF.
The Sidereal may commit a single Charge to increase the corresponding (Ability) and (College) to the highest of the two, this does not count as dice added by Charms.
Only one Least (College) Alignment may be active at a time.
This Charm is not considered an Astrological Charm, and as such doesn't cause massive disturbances to Fate.
